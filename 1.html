Head

The head is the upper part of the human body. It supports the face and is maintained by the skull, which encloses the brain. The head includes several key structures:

  • Skull: Protects the brain and supports the structures of the face.
  • Eyes: Organs of vision that allow us to see the world around us.
  • Ears: Organs of hearing and balance.
  • Nose: Organ of smell and a passageway for air into the respiratory system.
  • Mouth: Opening for food intake and speech production, containing teeth and tongue.
  • Brain: Control center of the body, responsible for thought, memory, emotion, and sensory processing.

Each part of the head plays a critical role in the overall functioning of the human body.
