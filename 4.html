Right Leg

The right leg is also crucial for mobility and support, mirroring the components of the left leg:

  • Femur: Thigh bone, the longest and strongest bone in the body.
  • Tibia and Fibula: Lower leg bones that support body weight and provide attachment points for muscles.
  • Knee Joint: Enables bending and straightening of the leg, supported by ligaments and cartilage.
  • Quadriceps and Hamstrings: Muscles that power leg movement, including walking, running, and jumping.
